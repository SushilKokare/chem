To determine the concerntration in terms of molarity of KMnO4 bt titrating it against (0.1M) standard solution of oxalic acid

Procedure

  • Drag the distilled water to the burette to wash it. Done
  • Drag the KMnO4 bottle on the burette to fill it. Done
  • Drag the Pipette on oxalic acid and collect 10ml of oxalic acid. Done
  • Drag the Pipette on the conocal flask to add the oxalic acid in it. Done
  • Drag the test tube containing Sulphuric acid to conical flask to add in it. Done
  • Click on the spirit lamp to get on. Done
  • Drag the test conical flask on the spirit lamp to heat and wait for a moment. Done
  • Drag the heated conical flask under the burette . Done
  • Click on the burette nozzel to add the KMnO4 solution in it. Done
